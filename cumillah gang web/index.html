History of Cumillah Gang

How the Meetup Happened

It all started with casual gatherings of friends sharing a passion for adventure. These meetups evolved, bringing together like-minded individuals.

How the Name Came About

The name 'Cumillah' symbolizes unity and strength, representing our commitment to stand by each other through challenges.
